Features

Everything in one place.

For anyone who looks at things before they buy, and keeps losing track of what they found.

Price Tracking
Price History Timelines
Tell the app what you're willing to pay. It checks every 15 minutes and builds a chart of how the price has moved over time. When it hits your number, you get a push notification. See the full history so you know whether today's price is actually a deal or just average.
Organization
Smart Collections
Group scans into folders like "Kitchen Remodel" or "BBQ Gear." Long-press any scan to add it. Filter your whole history down to one project in a single tap.
Receipts
Receipt Scanning
Point your camera at a paper receipt. The app pulls out the store name, the date, and every item on the list. No typing anything.
Purchase Tracking

When you buy something, mark it. Log the retailer, price, and date. Builds a real record of what you've actually spent — separate from items you're still watching.

What It Reads

It gets the details right

The app reads labels, text printed on packaging, logos, and product structure to get the exact name and model — not just "headphones" or "coffee maker." That precision is what makes price history and search actually useful.

  • Brand, model, and version — not just the category
  • Reads barcodes and product codes from the label
  • Pulls every line item off a paper receipt
Where It Saves

Your history goes with you, everywhere

Everything saves to your private account automatically. It works without Wi-Fi and syncs when you're back online. Price checks run on a schedule in the background — your phone doesn't need to be open for it to work.

  • Works offline, syncs when you reconnect
  • Price checks every 15 minutes, automatically
  • Checks more often the closer a price is to your target
  • Your data stays yours — export or delete any time
