Built to Remember

Two AI layers work together — one to identify what you're looking at with pixel-level precision, one to keep your entire shopping history organized, searchable, and useful over time.

🧠

Identification Layer

Gemini Vision performs a pixel-level audit on every scan — analyzing product details, OCR text, logos, and visual structure to nail the exact product, not just the category. Precise identification is the foundation that makes price tracking and history search actually useful.

  • Brand + model + generation precision
  • OCR-assisted product code extraction
  • Receipt parsing with line-item accuracy
💾

Memory Layer

Every scan is persisted to your personal Supabase history with full-text search indexing. Background price monitoring runs on a scheduled cadence against live shopping APIs, so your alerts are always fresh — without draining your battery or data.

  • Full-text search across your entire history
  • Scheduled background price polling
  • Offline-first with automatic cloud sync
